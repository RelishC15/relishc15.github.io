Peraqua

It is a water town themed commercial real estate project built in Hangzhou, including outlet shopping area, water purification Museum and campsite. We are committed to making peraqua a landmark in Hangzhou. In Peraqua, you can feel the gentle style of Jiangnan water town and visit the process of water purification, and feel the unique shopping experience.

Hibiscus

Hibiscus is a commercial-scenic integration project in Chengdu. The overall design of Hibiscus is youth-oriented, capturing the local customs and culture of Chengdu within the shopping center, creating a diverse and vibrant space exclusively for young customers with rich business formats.
